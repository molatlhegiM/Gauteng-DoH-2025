Gauteng DoH 2025

Africa’s Healthcare Future Starts Here

This is where Gauteng’s legacy of care meets bold new innovation. For over a decade, Gauteng has been at the center of Africa’s health development. In 2025, the Gauteng Department of Health Conference and Expo brings together public health leaders, pioneering minds, and global visionaries. Hosted in Sandton — the province’s beating heart — the event will showcase breakthrough ideas, inspire new partnerships, and define the next chapter of healthcare transformation across South Africa and beyond.

A new experience

Gauteng DoH 2025 marks a new era for healthcare advancement in South Africa. This groundbreaking event brings together policy-makers, healthcare professionals, innovators, and industry leaders to explore bold solutions that improve public health outcomes. With cutting-edge exhibits and impactful dialogue, Gauteng DoH 2025 is where Africa’s healthcare future is shaped — driven by local excellence and global collaboration.
